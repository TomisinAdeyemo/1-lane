Africa Now: August 2025 (DJ Mix)
DJ Hacord

Listen Now

DJ Hacord (born Biodun Bilahu) is a vibrant and versatile DJ whose journey began at a young age with a deep-rooted passion for music and local street events. After receiving professional training in 2012, he transitioned into the professional DJ scene, quickly earning recognition for his dynamic mixes and exceptional song curation. His distinctive sound blends Afrobeats, Hip Hop, Amapiano, R&B, and EDM reflecting his versatility and wide-ranging musical influences. His latest release, “Bad Girl” featuring Shakes and Jaethevert, further showcases his ability to craft fresh, engaging sounds that resonate with today’s audiences. Renowned for his refined turntable skills and genre-bending sets, DJ Hacord continues to captivate listeners and build momentum, even without live performances yet. Committed to spreading joy through music, he remains dedicated to evolving his craft and leaving a lasting imprint on the industry.

Exclusive DJ Mix
NO S.I.J, NO PARTY

Listen Now

Adedamola Sijuade known as DJ S.I.J, born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, discovered his love for music early on, inspired by the rich sounds of afrobeats, he began his DJ journey back home from Hosting and performing at friends' events and house parties before moving to Canada in 2023, where he officially launched his professional career. Shortly after arriving, S.I.J secured his first residency at 6ix Lounge in May 2023, quickly building a reputation for electrifying sets that keep crowds on their feet. His talent and energy have earned him spots playing for major artists like Davido, Mayorkun, Asake, BNXN, DJ Tunez, and more. In 2025, he took things even further by landing a second residency at Vibes Lounge — one of the biggest afrobeats hotspots in the city — solidifying his status as a go-to DJ for high-energy, unforgettable parties. Beyond residencies, DJ S.I.J has performed at countless private gigs, including weddings, bridal showers, birthday parties, and exclusive events. Known for his versatile mix of afrobeats, amapiano, dancehall, R&B, and hip hop, DJ S.I.J continues to push boundaries and connect people through music, always delivering on his promise: No S.I.J, No Party
