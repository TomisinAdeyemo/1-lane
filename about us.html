ABOUT US

1LaNe (1 Lift artists ‘N’ empower) is a full-service entertainment company dedicated to empowering artists from the ground up. We provide tailored marketing, distribution, and sync services, equipping creatives with the knowledge and resources they need to thrive in the evolving music industry. We refine raw talents by guiding them through essential artist development stages, ensuring they evolve with clarity and purpose. Our mission is to support artists in building sustainable careers while making informed decisions that align with their long-term goals. 1LaNe is committed to nurturing talent and delivering impactful creative solutions through a comprehensive and integrated approach.
