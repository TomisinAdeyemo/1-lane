ABOUT US

1LaNe (1 Lift artists ‘N’ empower) is a full-service entertainment company dedicated to uplifting and empowering artists. We provide tailored marketing, artist development, distribution, and sync services, equipping creatives with the knowledge and resources they need to thrive in the evolving music industry. Our mission is to support artists in building sustainable careers while ensuring they make informed decisions that align with their long-term goals. 1LaNe is committed to fostering talent and delivering impactful creative solutions through a comprehensive and integrated approach.
